Hello! I’m Abhinav Java, a Pre-Doctoral Research Fellow at Microsoft Research India . I work at the intersection of retrieval-augmented generation and LLM reasoning, advised by Dr. Srivathsan Koundinyan, Dr. Amit Sharma, and Dr. Nagarajan Natarajan. My current projects include:

  • Efficient multi-hop QA: fine-tuning small LMs on 1,000 examples to solve complex, chained queries.
  • Verifiable reward models for long-form answer generation in language models.
  • “Deep research” benchmarks for reasoning over scientific and technical texts (preprint & benchmark coming soon).
  • Robustness analysis of vision foundation models in collaboration with Prof. Vineeth N. Balasubramanian (IISc).

Before joining MSR-India, I spent two years as a Research Associate at Adobe Media & Data Science Lab , where I collaborated with Prof. Chirag Agarwal and Dr. Balaji Krishnamurthy on:

  • Responsible deployment of foundation models (NAACL 2025, AAAI 2024, ECCV 2022).
  • Multimodal learning and exemplar-based image editing (WACV 2025, WACV 2023).
  • Bias mitigation and privacy in large language and vision models.

I graduated in July 2022 with a B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering from Delhi Technological University. During undergrad, I worked with MIT Media Lab’s Camera Culture Group on privacy-preserving vision, Adobe on document intelligence, and IIT Bombay on low-resource OCR systems.

For the latest details, see my CV. Feel free to reach out via email.
