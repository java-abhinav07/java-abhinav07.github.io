I am a Pre-Doctoral Research Fellow at Microsoft Research India , where I work with Dr. Srivathsan Koundinyan, Dr. Amit Sharma, and Dr. Nagarajan Natarajan. I’m keen to explore problems at the intersection of reliable and efficient methods for training foundation models to enhance their real-world robustness. My overarching goal is to enable their safe and effective use in high-stakes applications.

Our latest work, accepted at the ICML 2025 EsFoMo Workshop, presents a lightweight and sample-efficient method for multi-hop question answering using small language models (arXiv).

I also collaborate with Prof. Vineeth N. Balasubramanian on benchmarking robustness of vision foundation models on various perception tasks. Previously, I was a Research Associate at Adobe Media & Data Science Lab, where I worked on multimodal learning (\href{https://reedit-diffusion.github.io/}{WACV 2025}, \href{https://reedit-diffusion.github.io/}{WACV 2023}) and responsible deployment of foundation models (\href{https://aclanthology.org/2025.naacl-long.416/}{NAACL 2025}, \href{https://arxiv.org/abs/2311.05451}{AAAI 2024}).

I received my B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from Delhi Technological University in 2022, where I worked with the MIT Media Lab’s Camera Culture Group on privacy-preserving vision systems (\href{https://arxiv.org/abs/2203.12192}{ECCV 2022}).
